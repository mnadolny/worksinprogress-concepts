Works in Progress · Interaction concepts Vol. 01 — Concept deck

Six signature moments
for a reading-first redesign.

A short pitch deck of working prototypes for a Works in Progress redesign concept. Each demonstrates one editorial moment — a hero treatment, a typographic flourish, a way to handle pullquotes, footnotes, reading progress, and inline galleries. Take them one at a time, or jump to the seventh card to see all six stitched into a single demo article. Best on desktop; mobile fallbacks included.

01 Ready

Receding hero

A full-bleed illustration that pushes back in z-space as you scroll, the headline floating up over it.

02 Ready

Animated drop cap

The opening letter draws itself in stroke-first, then fills — the page composing around it.

03 Ready

Pullquote takeover

A pullquote that scales up to claim the full viewport, holds a beat, then releases back into flow.

04 Coming

Footnote hover-card

Hover a footnote and a small, typeset card appears beside it. Click to pin, mobile taps to expand.

05 Coming

Composed progress bar

Not a thin line — a film-scrubber for the essay, with section markers, footnote ticks, and figure positions.

06 Coming

Horizontal inline gallery

Vertical scroll converts to horizontal across a sequence of images, then returns to the page.

07 — Demo article Coming

All six, stitched into one essay.

The leave-behind. A complete sample article using each interaction in its natural position — the URL the client returns to after the meeting.
